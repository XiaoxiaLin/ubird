About Ubird

Fitness landscapes depict how genotypes manifest at the phenotypic level and form the basis of our understanding of many areas of biology. However, many properties of fitness landscapes remain elusive. This website was created to help scientists study the fitness landscape of their protein of choice. While visualizing the data quality, it also predicts mutations, which do not influence the fitness of the protein.

File Upload

The file format should be the following:

Each mutant should be represented by a row. The columns should be arranged in the following manner:
  • List of mutations *
  • Number of barcodes corresponding to each mutant
  • The value of fitness
  • The fitness standard deviation
* The mutations, contained within each mutant, should be separated by colon. Each mutation should be represented by a single letter code of the initial amino acid, the position of the mutation within the protein and the amino acid it was changed into. Therefore, if we have a mutant, which consists of two mutations: one from alanine to tryptophan in the position 71, and another one from lysine to cysteine in the position 147, we would write: A71W:K147C.
